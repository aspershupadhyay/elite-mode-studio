Features

Everything you need to create great content

No subscription. No cloud. Your ideas stay on your computer.

✍️

AI Post Generator

Pick a topic, choose your tone and platform, and get a complete caption in seconds. Works for Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter, and more.

🎨

Design Studio

A drag-and-drop canvas like Figma, right inside the app. Add text, shapes, images, and frames. Snap guides and rulers are built in.

🌐

AI Web Search

The app searches the web for fresh, relevant information before writing your post. No more outdated or generic content.

📄

Document Mode

Upload a PDF or document and use it as context for generation. Great for writing posts about your own research or reports.

🖼️

8 Pro Templates

Start from a professionally designed template: Dark Minimal, Bold Gradient, Neon Dark, LinkedIn Banner, Story format, and more.

📚

Post History

Every post you generate is automatically saved. Go back anytime to copy, edit, or send it to the design canvas.

🤖

AI Profiles

Create different profiles for different accounts. Each profile has its own tone, platform, posting style, and instructions.

🔒

Fully Private

Everything runs on your own computer. Your content, your API keys, your data. Nothing is sent to any third-party service.

How it works

From idea to post in 4 steps

It is really this simple. Even if you have never used AI tools before.

1

Download and set up

Download the app and paste in your free NVIDIA and Tavily API keys. Takes about 2 minutes.

2

Type your topic

Just describe what you want to post about. "5 tips for waking up early" or "news about AI this week" both work.

3

Pick your style

Choose your platform (Instagram, LinkedIn, etc.), tone (funny, professional, casual), and how many posts you want.

4

Generate and design

Click Generate. Your posts appear in seconds. Send them straight to the Design Studio to make graphics and carousels.

Mac users: getting a security warning?

macOS may say it cannot verify the app. Just right-click the app icon in Finder and choose Open. Click Open in the popup. That is it, it will work fine after that.

Setup

Two free API keys required

Both are free to get and take under 2 minutes to set up.

1

NVIDIA API Key

This powers all the AI writing. Go to build.nvidia.com, create a free account, and generate an API key. It starts with nvapi-

2

Tavily API Key

This lets the app search the web for fresh information. Go to app.tavily.com, sign up free, and copy your key. The free plan is more than enough.

3

Paste into the app

When you open CreatorOS for the first time it will ask for both keys. Paste them in and you are ready to go. You can update them anytime in Settings.
